Who we are

A new standard for marine service contracts.

Whitestone Partners LLC is a boat service contract company based in St. George, Utah. We partner with certified marine dealers to deliver comprehensive annual maintenance contracts to boat owners.

Our program is dealer-first by design. We handle the contracts, billing, and administration. Dealers handle the work. Boat owners get peace of mind. Everyone wins.

Terms & Conditions
Please read this agreement carefully before registering your boat.
1. Contract Overview

Whitestone Partners LLC offers annual boat maintenance contracts covering ten (10) core services performed by our certified dealer network.

2. Covered Services

Your contract covers: Summer Prep, Impeller Service, Engine Oil Service, Fuel Filter Service, Transmission Oil Service, Outdrive Service, Shaft Alignment, Winterization, V-Drive Service, and Ballast Cartridge Service. Service eligibility is based on engine hours and seasonal requirements.

3. Exclusions

Not covered: misuse or neglect, accident damage, major parts replacement, non-listed services, commercial vessels, pre-existing conditions.

4. Dealer Network

All covered services must be performed by a Whitestone Partners certified dealer.

5. Cancellation & Refunds

Cancellation and refund terms will be provided at time of purchase.

6. Limitation of Liability

Total liability shall not exceed the original contract value.

7. Disputes

Disputes go to good-faith negotiation first; unresolved within 30 days proceed to binding arbitration.
