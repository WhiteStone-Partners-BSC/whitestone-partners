Who we are

A new standard for marine service contracts.

Whitestone Partners LLC is a boat service contract company based in Utah. We partner with certified marine dealers to deliver comprehensive annual maintenance contracts to boat owners.

Our program is dealer-first by design. We handle the contracts, billing, and administration. Dealers handle the work. Boat owners get peace of mind. Everyone wins.
